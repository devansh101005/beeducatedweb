# Be Educated Home Tutor - Website

Welcome to the **Be Educated Home Tutor** project! This website is designed to connect students and tutors for seamless online and in-person education experiences.

---

## 📚 About the Project

**Be Educated Home Tutor** is a platform that:

- Helps students find qualified tutors.
- Allows teachers to join and showcase their expertise.
- Simplifies fee management, attendance, and scheduling.
- Offers easy access to study materials, alerts, and updates.

---

## 🚀 Key Features

✅ **Home Page**  
- Logo, tagline, short intro, highlights.  
- Call-to-action buttons for demo classes and tutor registration.  
- Testimonials and contact info.

✅ **Student & Teacher Registration**  
- Separate forms to collect relevant details.

✅ **Login Dashboards**  
- Tailored dashboards for students, teachers, and admin.  
- Schedule, attendance, and payment controls.

✅ **Attendance & Fee Management**  
- Teachers mark attendance, admin monitors reports.  
- Online/offline fee options, auto reminders, and receipts.

✅ **Study Materials**  
- Upload/download PDFs, docs, and images.  
- Class-wise and subject-wise materials.

✅ **Class Scheduling & Notifications**  
- Timetables with alerts/reminders via WhatsApp/SMS/Email.

✅ **Testimonials & Results**  
- Parent/student feedback, results showcase, top student highlights.

✅ **About Us & Contact Us**  
- Story, vision, mission, team intro.  
- Contact form, Google Maps, phone/email/WhatsApp.

✅ **Blog & SEO**  
- Articles on study tips, exam prep, parenting advice.  
- Updates on new classes/batches.

✅ **Security & User Experience**  
- OTP login, mobile responsiveness, SSL security.

✅ **Future-Ready Add-ons**  
- Mobile app, video classes, LMS, performance tracking.
